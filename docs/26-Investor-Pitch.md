**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Target Audience:** Angel Investors, Seed Venture Capitalists  
**Last Updated:** July 2026  

---

## 1. Executive Summary
**AI Language Coach** is an AI-powered language training platform that helps learners prepare for high-stakes language proficiency exams (including IELTS, PTE, TOEFL, OET, and Goethe). By combining real-time voice conversations, structured grammar parsing, native language (L1 Malayalam) scaffolding, and adaptive study plans, the platform delivers the quality of a personal language tutor at a fraction of the cost.

*   ** vision:** To become the world's leading AI-powered language learning companion for students, professionals, and immigrants.
*   ** mission:** Replace expensive offline coaching classes with an accessible, personalized, and encouraging AI coach available 24/7.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Millions of learners preparing for international education, immigration, or jobs face major barriers:
1.  **Expensive Coaching:** Traditional offline training classes cost between **$150 and $500/month**, which is unaffordable for many learners.
2.  **Scarcity of Speaking Partners:** Learners lack opportunities to practice speaking, which is critical for oral exams.
3.  **Delayed Feedback:** Essay grading and speaking feedback are often delayed, generic, and inconsistent.
4.  **Language Barriers:** Learners struggle with complex grammar rules explained only in English, requiring native-language (e.g., Malayalam) scaffolding.

---

## 3. Solution

An AI companion that offers:

*   **24/7 AI Tutor:** Real-time conversational tutoring powered by Emma, David, and other specialized personas.
*   **Live Voice Conversations:** Low-latency conversational practice using LiveKit WebRTC.
*   **Grammar Engine:** Detailed, inline grammatical feedback.
*   **Pronunciation Analysis:** Word-level clarity and intonation feedback.
*   **Malayalam Scaffolding:** Grammatical rules explained in Malayalam, fading out as the user's proficiency improves.
*   **Timed Exam Simulators:** Full IELTS, PTE, and Goethe mock tests.
*   **Personalized Study Plans:** 30-day adaptive study guides.
*   **Weekly Progress Reports:** Detailed analytical dashboards tracking learner performance.

---

## 4. Target Audience

### Primary Market:
*   **Immigrants & Students:** Candidates preparing for IELTS, PTE, TOEFL, and OET exams.
*   **Professionals:** Nurses, doctors, and engineers seeking jobs in English- or German-speaking countries.

### Secondary Market:
*   **B2B Institutions:** Language academies, colleges, and university study-abroad departments seeking software tools to scale their coaching capabilities.

## 6. Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
1.  **Exam Specialization:** Unlike general chatbots, our AI evaluates speaking and writing tasks using official IELTS/PTE rubrics.
2.  **Malayalam L1 Scaffolding:** Explains grammar rules and vocabulary in Malayalam, with translation support decreasing as the user's CEFR level climbs.
3.  **Stateless API Cost Control:** Edge Function prompt strategies optimize model routing, managing API costs without reducing quality.

---

## 7. Business Model & Revenue Tiers

We use a Freemium subscription model with additional in-app purchase options:
*   **Free Plan (Ad-Supported):** 50 text messages/day, 10 voice minutes/day, and 1 mock exam/week.
*   **Premium Plan ($15/month or $120/year):** Unlimited text and voice practice, full writing evaluations, mock exams, and personalized RAG memory.
*   **Institutional B2B Licensing:** Subscriptions for language academies and schools to manage and track student performance.
*   **Writing Feedback Credits:** In-app purchases for additional essay evaluations.

---

## 8. Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy
*   **Phase 1 (Months 1-3) - Launch & Local Growth:** Target IELTS/OET coaching centers in Kerala, India, using Malayalam-to-English tutoring as a key differentiator.
*   **Phase 2 (Months 4-6) - GCC & ME Expansion:** Expand to Middle East countries with large nursing and professional expat populations.
*   **Phase 3 (Months 7-12) - Global Scale:** Expand to Europe, Canada, and Australia. Leverage influencer campaigns, SEO, and partnerships with study-abroad agencies.

## 11. Funding Requirements (Pre-Seed Ask: $250,000)
*   **Product Development (40% - $100k):** Complete mobile app development, speaking/writing evaluations, and timed mock exam modules.
*   **AI Infrastructure (20% - $50k):** Prompt engineering, model routing optimizations, and LiveKit Cloud streaming costs.
*   **Marketing & Customer Acquisition (25% - $62.5k):** Social media ads, influencer partnerships, and local school marketing.
*   **Operations & Hiring (15% - $37.5k):** Legal compliance, cloud hosting, and language expert advisors.

---

## 12. Risks & Mitigations

*   **Risk:** High API costs for LLM tokens and text-to-speech.
    *   *Mitigation:* Cache grammar rules, use lightweight models (Gemini Flash) for casual chat, and apply strict rate limits for free tiers.
*   **Risk:** Rapidly changing model landscapes and new AI features from competitors.
    *   *Mitigation:* Abstract model routing so we can switch providers easily, and focus on niche exam prep and native language scaffolding.

---

## 13. Exit Opportunities
*   **EdTech Acquisition:** Acquisition by established language learning companies (such as Duolingo, ELSA Speak, or Babbel) seeking exam-specific AI tools.
*   **SaaS Growth:** Scaling into a profitable, independent SaaS business.
